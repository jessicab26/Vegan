Vegan Recipes

The most important part of any recipe is the ingredients!

Now, please keep in mind that there is a wide range of ingredients for you to use with your recipes. This list is an overall generalization of just a few ingredients that are useful to have on hand while cooking a Vegan or Vegetarian meal.

  1. Beans or Legumes
  2. Grains
  3. Tempeh
  4. Tofu
  5. Nuts/Seeds
  6. Dried fruits and fruits
  7. Vegetable stock
  8. Nutritional yeast
  9. Miso
  10. Tahini
  11. Dried Sea Vegetables
  12. Coconut Oil
  13. Maple syrup
  14. Ground flax seeds
