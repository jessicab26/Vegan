So you want to become a Vegan?

Or at least, you want to begin a more Vegetarian-Friendly diet?
Where to start?

I'm not going to lie. . . Cutting meat from your daily diet can be tough! I'm from the South, and if you didn't grill BBQ or stew a pot roast at least once every month, you weren't a true Southerner. And with a Chick-Fil-A on just about every corner, how can you resist?

And, when you start counting the numerous other animal by-products in our daily diet, it can become overwhelming!

Do not worry!

There are countless websites and guides and recipes out there to help you get started!

First, let's talk a little bit about what Veganism is. . .

"Veganism is a philosophy and way of living which seeks to exclude—as far as is possible and practicable—all forms of exploitation of, and cruelty to, animals for food, clothing or any other purpose; and by extension, promotes the development and use of animal-free alternatives for the benefit of animals, humans and the environment. In dietary terms it denotes the practice of dispensing with all products derived wholly or partly from animals." - per The Vegan Society
